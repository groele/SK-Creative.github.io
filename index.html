About Me

I am a physicist focused on two-dimensional materials, excitonic physics, and nanophotonics. My research integrates experimental optoelectronics and quantum materials engineering.

Research Interests

  • Van der Waals Heterostructures
  • Exciton–Trion Conversion in TMDs
  • Strain and Plasmonic Engineering
  • Quantum Photonics and Imaging
